Vegan Moroccan Chickpea and Apricot

Vegan Moroccan Chickpea and Apricot is a delightful fusion of sweet and savory flavors, packed with warm spices and wholesome ingredients. Tender chickpeas and juicy apricots are simmered in a fragrant sauce infused with cinnamon, cumin, and ginger, creating a rich and comforting dish. Slow-cooked with tomatoes, onions, and garlic, this Moroccan-inspired recipe delivers a perfect balance of heartiness and natural sweetness.

Chicken Broccoli Tomato Carbonara

Chicken Broccoli Tomato Carbonara is a creamy, indulgent twist on the classic Italian favorite. Juicy chicken, crisp-tender broccoli, and sweet cherry tomatoes come together in a luscious, silky carbonara sauce made with eggs, Parmesan, and a hint of garlic.

Sesame Chicken Teriyaki Plate

Tender, juicy chicken glazed in a rich, house-made teriyaki sauce, sprinkled with toasted sesame seeds for the perfect crunch. Served over a bed of fluffy jasmine rice and paired with crisp stir-fried vegetables, this bowl is a perfect balance of sweet, savory, and umami flavors.
